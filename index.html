About Us

In 1985 after helping his brother save the Mushroom Kingdom, Luigi embarked on his own culinary journey. Traveling across Italy, leaning from world renown chefs, Luigi endevoured to start his own resturant, and share his cooking with the world.

Always using the freshest of ingredients, Luigi's Italiano Resturanto exceeds in delivering quility dishes, and providing guests with an unmatched fine dining experience.
